Self-Guided Journey · ~30 Days

Self-Guided HR

A structured, self-driven HR setup with everything clients need to launch confidently — at their own pace.

What Is a Self-Guided HR Implementation?

Self-Guided HR Implementation is designed for clients who want to set up their Employment Hero HR platform at their own pace, using structured digital resources instead of a dedicated consultant.

Clients receive a clear, step-by-step HR Milestone Plan, unlimited access to digital how-to guides and help articles, live chat access to our implementation team, and a 30-day guided timeline from kickoff to go-live.

How To Position This With Your Clients

"Employment Hero provides a Self-Guided Implementation for HR. You'll follow a structured milestone plan with clear instructions, videos and articles. You can complete the setup at your own pace, and if you need help along the way, you can reach the team via live chat."

👤

Option 1

Partner Leads

If you're setting up Employment Hero on behalf of your client, notify the EH team at point of sale. You'll be added as the primary point of contact and follow the same scope and plan as the client.

🏢

Option 2

Client Leads

Help clients understand the implementation is self-guided, what support is included, their responsibility to progress through the milestone plan, and the 30-day launch expectation.

What Clients Can Expect

1. Kickoff Milestone Plan

Within 2 business days of submitting billing details, the client receives Vitally access and their implementation plan. The plan outlines key setup steps, recommended order, due dates, and clear instructions for each stage.

2. Digital Implementation Resources

Clients receive unlimited access to Employment Hero's library of digital guides covering every HR module — step-by-step how-tos, video walkthroughs, best practice setup tips, and short articles for troubleshooting.

3. HR Milestone Plan Structure

The milestone plan provides a clear sequence of HR setup tasks, milestones for key stages (platform configuration, employee setup, policies, onboarding), a visual representation of progress, and the expected timeline to reach launch.

4. What's Included

  • Unlimited access to digital resources
  • Structured HR Milestone Plan
  • Unlimited access to Live Chat (real-time support)
  • Expert-backed digital guidance

What's Not Included

A Self-Guided HR Implementation does not include: a dedicated implementation consultant, 1:1 training sessions, advisory services (award interpretation, legislative guidance), system configuration completed for the client, custom rule building outside pre-built Modern Awards, or content creation/upload (policies, contracts, LMS content).

What If the Client Needs Help?

While the process is self-guided, clients still have unlimited access to Live Chat, where they can speak directly with a HR implementation specialist.

  • Ask questions about HR setup tasks
  • Troubleshoot blockers in real time
  • Receive links to relevant help articles
  • Confirm whether they are following the correct process
  • Clarify milestones or setup requirements

Clients can use the live chat widget within the Employment Hero platform. Full instructions are in the Help Centre.

Consultant-Led · 4–26 Weeks

Managed Implementation

Employment Hero's consultant-led onboarding experience — for complex, high-touch clients who need specialist-led configuration and hands-on go-live support.

What Is a Managed Implementation?

A Managed Implementation is Employment Hero's consultant-led onboarding experience, designed to support customers with more complex, detailed or high-touch requirements than a self-guided journey can accommodate. Customers collaborate directly with an EH Project Team who guide them through each stage of setup.

Across all tiers, the purpose is the same: reduce complexity, provide specialist-led configuration, ensure a smooth and confident go-live, and support customers through testing, validation and rollout.

This model is ideal for organisations with multiple entities, awards, integrations, complex internal processes, or those who simply prefer hands-on support.

How To Position This With Your Clients

"With a Managed Implementation, Employment Hero's project team works directly with you to configure your platform, guide you through the project stages and support you through testing and go-live. It's a collaborative delivery model designed to reduce complexity and ensure a smooth, confident launch."

👤

Option 1

Partner Leads

Notify the EH team at point of sale. You'll be added as the primary point of contact, supported throughout by the Managed Implementation Team.

🏢

Option 2

Client Leads

Help clients understand the project structure, required commitments, what support is included, and the timeline. Setting clear expectations helps projects stay on track.

What Clients Can Expect

1. Clear Project Stages & Timeline

All Managed Implementations follow the same high-level structure:

  • Project Kick Off & Data Gathering — Implementation discovery calls, data extracts and platform creation
  • System Build & Configuration — Configuration of the platform and commencement of training
  • System Testing — Internal testing, customer testing, system walkthroughs
  • Pre-Launch Preparation & Go-Live — Final activities, rollout, live run assistance, project closure

Duration is influenced by data return timelines, platform complexity, customer availability, number of entities/employees, and integrations or award complexities.

2. Collaborative Build & Configuration

The EH Project Team supports clients to transform and finalise employee data, configure key platform settings, set up required HR and/or Payroll components, and begin self-led training modules.

3. Structured Testing Before Launch

Clients work with the EH Project Team to review system setup, test HR and/or Payroll workflows, validate rules, settings and processes, ensure sync between HR and Payroll (if applicable), and complete customer testing with guidance.

4. Guided Go-Live Support

During Go-Live, the project team helps complete HR rollout and launch planning, pre-Go-Live payroll activities (Chart of Accounts, ATO/IRD settings, YTD earnings, historical gross earnings), opening balances, system rollout, a live payroll run (for payroll subscribers), and final review.

How Support Works

Throughout the Managed Implementation, clients have access to their EH Project Team who lead delivery and configuration, pre-recorded training modules, the Employment Hero Help Centre, and specialists during testing and go-live. Once the project concludes, customers transition to our Support Team for ongoing assistance.

Post-Sale,
Pre-Implementation

What happens between your client signing and implementation kicking off — and how you can set them up to start strong.

⚡ Applies to Self-Guided Implementations only
⚠️

No platform access will be granted until billing information has been submitted and verified. Processing time can be up to 2 business days. Platform emails will not be sent until billing is confirmed.

Platform Access: Delivered in Two Parts

Each platform your client has signed up for (HR, Payroll, Vitally) is provisioned independently — with its own provisioning process and timing. When a platform is ready, logins are delivered in two separate emails:

📧

Email 1

Business Access Email

Sent to the nominated user's inbox. This is the first email the client will receive for each platform.

🔑

Email 2

Login Details Email

Sent from a no-reply address. Access isn't available until both emails are received. Remind clients to check their spam folders.

Email 1 — Business Access Email
Email 2 — Login Details Email

Important: Using the password reset functionality will not work until both emails have been received. Clients often assume they're ready to log in after the first email — remind them to wait for both.

Provisioning Is Staggered Across Teams

Employment Hero uses dedicated provisioning teams for each product stream — HR platform, Payroll platform, and Vitally onboarding hub. As a result:

  • Clients may receive access to platforms in different orders
  • Some platforms may be provisioned faster than others
  • It is normal for access to arrive out of sequence

Clients should wait until they have received access to all required platforms before attempting to begin setup. Starting prematurely can cause misalignment and onboarding delays.

Only Nominated Users Receive Access

Platform access is provided only to nominated contacts. Common client misunderstandings to avoid:

  • Resetting a password for an account that doesn't exist won't work
  • Access is not shared — logins are individual and cannot be forwarded
  • New users must be formally added through the correct channel

Help Your Clients Start Strong

Reinforce these 5 points with every referred client:

  • 1. Billing details must be submitted before access begins
  • 2. Platform access is sent in two separate emails per product
  • 3. Different platforms are provisioned by different teams, on different timelines
  • 4. Setup should only begin once all access is confirmed
  • 5. Only nominated users will receive login details

Vitally Overview

Your client's central onboarding workspace — where their implementation plan, tasks, and progress all live.

What is Vitally?

Vitally is Employment Hero's onboarding workspace. For clients going through a Self-Guided Implementation, Vitally acts as their central project hub, step-by-step implementation plan, source of instructions and how-to guides, and place to track deadlines and progress.

How Vitally Supports Partners

When you refer a client, Vitally helps them:

  • Start quickly — They receive their onboarding plan within 2 business days
  • Stay on track — Clear steps, dates and checklists reduce overwhelm
  • Avoid delays — Everything needed (guides, links, instructions) is in one place
  • Get support — Clear guidance on where to go for help if needed

What Clients Can Expect

1. An Initial Email Invitation

The client will receive an email from Vitally with a link to their implementation workspace, including a welcome message, their personalised Self-Guided Implementation Plan, helpful links and resources, and instructions to get started right away.

⚠️ Because this email comes from the Vitally system, it may land in junk or spam. Encourage your clients to check these folders if they don't see it.

2. Their Vitally Document (Welcome Page)

This is the client's home base. It includes expectations for their onboarding, where to get support, links to additional information pages, and their Self-Guided Implementation Plan.

3. The Self-Guided Implementation Plan

The client's project plan outlining all required setup steps, the recommended order of tasks, due dates, and checkboxes to mark items as complete. Each task can be opened to reveal deeper instructions.

4. Detailed Task Information

Every task includes what the task is, where to complete it in the platform, why it matters, prerequisites, key tips, and helpful articles and links. This ensures clients understand the context, not just the action.

Who Gets Access to Vitally?

Vitally access is provisioned based on the implementation contacts nominated by the client in their Client Services Agreement (CSA). These contacts are automatically provisioned with access to both the Vitally workspace and the relevant Employment Hero platforms.

⚠️

If You're Supporting the Implementation: If you or a third party (accountant, bookkeeper, or internal team member) are assisting with the client's implementation, the client must nominate you as an implementation contact on their CSA. If this step is missed, you will not receive access by default — even if you're actively involved in the project.

Need Additional Access?

If someone else needs access to the Vitally workspace, an existing implementation contact can request it via Live Chat Support — simply provide the full name and email address of the person to be added.

How You Can Position Vitally With Your Clients

"Once Employment Hero activates your onboarding, you'll receive a Vitally plan. Think of it like your digital checklist — it guides you through each step with instructions, videos and links. It helps you move quickly and ensures nothing gets missed."

Encourage clients to: open the plan as soon as it arrives, bookmark their Vitally link, follow the task order, and reach out if they get stuck.

Implementation Fees

A clear breakdown of implementation pricing — and the golden rule every partner must know.

⚠️

Important: Partners Must Not Quote or Scope Implementations.

All implementation pricing is determined exclusively by Employment Hero after in-depth discovery with the client. Partners must not scope or diagnose implementation requirements, recommend a tier, or quote implementation pricing. Incorrect scoping can lead to delivery delays, compliance issues and poor client outcomes.

Self-Guided Implementation Fees

Self-Guided implementations are priced using a flat-fee rate, which varies by employee count and product selection.

Package < 10 employees < 30 employees > 30 employees
HR only $500 $1,000 $1,500
Payroll only $500 $1,000 $1,500
HR & Payroll / EOS $500 $1,000 $2,500

These fees reflect the onboarding resources, milestone plans, and ongoing support made available to the customer throughout their setup. ⚠️ All pricing is subject to minimums and may vary.

Managed Implementation Fees

Managed Implementations are priced using a per-employee rate, which varies by complexity and the level of support required.

Package Fees
HR only $100–$200 per employee
Payroll only $100–$200 per employee
HR & Payroll / EOS $100–$200 per employee

Pricing varies by complexity. The per-employee rate within the range is scoped during discovery and proposal. ⚠️ All pricing is subject to minimums and may vary.

How To Position This With Your Clients

"Employment Hero scopes all implementations directly with clients following discovery session(s). Each business has different HR and Payroll requirements, so Employment Hero will put forward the aligned implementation package and pricing based on your project complexity and support needs."

This approach allows you to remain supportive without ever stepping into scoping or quoting.

Implementation FAQ

The questions referral partners ask most — answered clearly, no fluff.

1. Who is responsible for scoping implementation needs? ▼

Employment Hero. All implementation complexity, tiering and pricing are assessed and confirmed by EH during the Discovery process.

2. Can partners quote or estimate implementation pricing? ▼

No. Partners must not scope, estimate or quote implementation fees. EH will always present the correct package and pricing directly to the client.

3. Are the implementation fees fixed? ▼

No. Fees are indicative and subject to change. Final pricing depends on complexity and is confirmed by EH after Discovery.

4. What factors influence the implementation tier and price? ▼

EH assesses:

  • Employee count and number of entities
  • HR and/or Payroll complexity
  • Awards, pay rules and data requirements
  • Internal workflows and processes
  • Integrations or system dependencies
  • Client capacity and readiness
5. What's the difference between Self-Guided and Managed? ▼

Self-Guided: Client follows a digital milestone plan and uses live chat for support.

Managed: EH consultants lead the project, run workshops, configure the system, support testing, and guide go-live.

6. Can partners choose which implementation a client needs? ▼

No. EH determines the correct implementation type and tier based on Discovery findings.

7. Can partners attend Discovery or Kick-Off calls? ▼

Yes. Partners can join where relevant to provide context and support the client, but EH owns scoping and delivery.

8. What's the partner's role during implementation? ▼
  • Support the client with communication and expectations
  • Reinforce the client's responsibilities (data, testing, deadlines)
  • Help keep the project on track
  • Refer any scope or pricing questions to EH
9. Who supports the client after go-live? ▼

Once implementation is completed, the client transitions to Employment Hero Support for ongoing assistance.

10. What if the client needs help during Self-Guided Implementation? ▼

They use live chat, which routes them to the correct HR or Payroll specialist. EOS clients choose which module their query relates to so they reach the right team.

11. Can partners troubleshoot or configure the platform for clients? ▼

Self-Guided: Partners may follow the self-guided implementation journey on behalf of a client.

Managed: Partners may participate in calls, provide context and support the client in meeting deadlines — but the implementation itself is delivered by EH.

12. What happens if a client's needs change mid-project? ▼

EH may re-scope the project or adjust the implementation tier. Additional fees may apply, and only EH manages this process.

Become an Approved
Implementation Partner

Deliver implementations for your own referred clients with fees waived — once you've proven you're ready.

Some partners are interested in delivering the Employment Hero implementation themselves for their referred clients, with implementation fees waived. We're open to supporting this pathway, and when done well, it can streamline onboarding and strengthen your expertise with the platform.

⚠️

Important: Where implementation fees are waived, the partner agrees to deliver the implementation independently, without access to Employment Hero's implementation training resources or support from the Implementation Team. If support or intervention is required at any stage, Employment Hero reserves the right to revoke approval and issue an invoice for the appropriate implementation journey.

Pathway to Approval

Contact your Partnership Manager to discuss next steps. They'll guide you through the requirements and support you throughout the approval process.

✅

Step 01

Demonstrated Experience

We require a minimum of five referred and successfully implemented client platforms delivered by you end-to-end. This builds confidence that you're familiar with the setup workflow, platform controls and project structure.

🔎

Step 02

Quality Check

Our team will review whether correct support channels were used, the number of escalations raised during implementation, any setup-related support required after go-live, and client sentiment and overall implementation experience.

📚

Step 03

Continuous Learning Commitment

Employment Hero releases new features every single week. Approved Implementation Partners are expected to undertake ongoing self-guided learning to stay across new features, updates and best practices.

Why This Matters

Our goal is to empower partners while ensuring clients always receive the right level of support, accuracy and consistency. When partners are approved, we know they're equipped to deliver great onboarding outcomes — and we can confidently waive fees knowing the client experience is protected.

Caveats for Approved Partners

Even once approved, there are still limits on what you can deliver. Approval does not extend to high-complexity payroll work, advanced award interpretation, bespoke configurations, enterprise setups, or modules outside your approved scope.

All fee-waived implementations require formal client consent. Employment Hero will continue to manage any requirements that carry compliance risk or sit outside standard implementation flows.

What This Looks Like in Practice

As an approved partner delivering a fee-waived implementation, the experience is fundamentally different from a standard self-guided or managed journey. Here's what you need to know before you start:

🚫

No Access

No Vitally / Implementation Documentation

Approved partners do not have access to Employment Hero's implementation workspace (Vitally) or any structured implementation documentation. There is no milestone plan, no guided task list, and no prescribed order of setup provided. You are responsible for determining how to sequence and manage the implementation.

📖

Available

Help Centre — Functionality Guidance Only

The Employment Hero Help Centre is available for reference. It covers platform functionality and feature-level guidance. It is not a substitute for implementation documentation and does not provide setup sequencing, configuration best practices, or project management support.

💬

Limited

Live Chat Connects to Support — Not Implementation

Live chat is available, but it routes to the Support team, not the Implementation team. Support can assist with platform functionality questions but cannot provide implementation guidance, setup sequencing advice, or project-level assistance.

🗓️

Flexible

No Go-Live Guidelines or Timeline Tracking

There are no prescribed go-live guidelines, timelines, or milestone targets for fee-waived implementations. You can complete the setup at your own pace — however, Employment Hero will not monitor progress, send reminders, or take any steps to ensure the project stays on track. Delivery accountability sits entirely with you.

⚠️

The above limitations are intentional and non-negotiable. If at any point implementation support, guidance, or intervention is required from Employment Hero, the fee waiver may be revoked and an invoice issued for the appropriate implementation journey. If you have any uncertainty about scope, speak to your Partnership Manager before proceeding.

Why it's required, what it covers, and the templates to use.

Why Client Consent Is Required

Even if you've implemented hundreds of clients, we still require explicit written consent from the end customer because the implementation directly affects their data, compliance settings, payroll configuration, workflows, and long-term platform experience.

As the account owner, the client must acknowledge who is making configuration decisions on their behalf and understand that, under a fee-waived arrangement, Employment Hero will not be providing implementation support or intervention.

This protects you, the client, and Employment Hero by ensuring everyone is aligned on responsibilities, boundaries, and ownership before the project begins.

Account Executive Email Template

Below is the example email our sales team will send to the client to obtain consent:

📧 AE Email Template

Hi [Client Name],

I hope you're doing well.

As we move toward finalising your agreement, the next step is to confirm your preferred implementation pathway for Employment Hero. I wanted to let you know that [Partner Business Name] has strong experience implementing the EH platform, and if you'd like them to lead your setup, we can look at waiving our standard self-guided implementation fees.

The choice really depends on how involved you'd like to be in the implementation. Some clients prefer to take a hands-on role, while others are more comfortable having their trusted partner manage the process end-to-end. We're happy to support whichever approach best suits your business.

To move forward with the partner-led option, we just need a quick written consent from you. I've popped a simple template below — feel free to copy it into your reply.

I confirm that [Client Business Name] authorises [Partner Business Name] to complete our Employment Hero implementation on our behalf.

We acknowledge and accept the following:

  • Implementation fees have been waived on the basis that the Partner delivers the full implementation independently.
  • Neither our team nor the Partner will receive support from the Employment Hero Implementation Team, nor access to implementation training resources.
  • Should implementation support be required at any stage, Employment Hero reserves the right to issue an invoice for any assistance provided.
  • The Partner is solely responsible for the accuracy, completeness, and appropriateness of all configuration completed during the implementation.

Once we receive this confirmation, we'll update your account and you'll be all set to move forward.

If you have any questions or need anything clarified, please let me know!

Warm regards,
